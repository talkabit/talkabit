LOCATION

The Site

Talk a Bit 2014 will once again take place at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto — a top-level engineering school, known to be one of the best in Europe, as well as in Portugal.

Arrival

You can arrive at Porto by car, bus or plane. To get to Faculdade de Engenharia da Faculdade de Porto you can take the yellow line of the subway (line D), and stop at IPO station, from there it is only a 5min walk in straigth line until you reach us. For more info regarding the subway network of Porto please check their website. If you want to come by cab, you can call Táxis Invicta.

Porto

Porto is one of the oldest tourist destinations in Europe and benefits from a privileged geographic location, complemented by a modern transport and communications network.

The richness of its monumental and artistic heritage, Port Wine, numerous leisure facilities and its cultural attractions invite you to visit this contemporary and inspired city well-known for its hospitality.​

If you’re staying in town beyond the conference, be sure to check out the historical center, visit some of the city’s multitude of museums like Serralves, stop by Casa da Música, take a cruise in Rio Douro, or try the traditional delicacies of the city, like Francesinha or Port Wine. Whatever you prefer, there’s something for everyone...
