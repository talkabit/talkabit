TALK A BIT 3rd Edition

Talk A Bit is an event organized by the students of Information Systems and Software Engineering Seminar, a class held at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto.

Address

Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto

Rua Dr. Roberto Frias, s/n 4200-465, Porto, Portugal

Location

Talk A Bit 2015 will once again take place at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto (FEUP) - a top-level engineering school, known to be one of the best in Europe, as well as in Portugal.

You can arrive at FEUP by car, bus (STCP) or subway (line D).
