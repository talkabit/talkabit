In recent years there have been several reports about negative impacts of AI in various domains of application, from criminal justice to healthcare, or finance.
Despite increasing awareness, the common practice has not yet evolved to consider fairness, accountability, and transparency as main objectives when building and deploying AI-powered software systems.
In this talk, I will give an overview of the current landscape in Responsible AI, discuss practical challenges, and share my experiences in both the public sector and in the fintech industry.
