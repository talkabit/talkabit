Remote has been gaining traction for a long time. Increasingly, companies around the world realize it's the future of work. But with it, many best practices and trends break down. Communicating and working synchronously is tricky when your team is distributed across the globe. Pair programming is hard, mob programming is harder, to name a few. So, how exactly do you collaborate effectively? How do you raise the quality bar as a team? How do you share knowledge, and keep people in the loop? Are there established and refined approaches from which we can draw inspiration?
