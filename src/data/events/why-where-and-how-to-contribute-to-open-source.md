Have you ever wondered how to contribute to open source projects and where to get started. In this presentation, I will provide insights over why it can be helpful for your career to contribute to Open Source, where you can get started with it (communities, initiatives, projects, etc) and how you can actually contribute in multiple ways to Open Source, whether you’re a developer, designer, tester… or just an interested person who wants to contribute to a project you care about.
