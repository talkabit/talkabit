Companies change over time: some stagnate, some adapt to the industry, others disrupt it and create new and unique ways of working. In this session, I'll share my view on how work in tech companies is changing, how companies are structured, how they think, and where you fit in (or not!). Now that you're almost a professional in the industry, do you think you have what it takes?
